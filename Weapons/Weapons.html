There are many weapons in Guts and Blackpowder, each being designed for a unique purpose, with most having some background history, this page will briefly explain and showcase the various weapons found in Guts and Blackpowder. More complex breakdowns and guides can be found on each weapon's specific pages. This page will cover firearms, melees, and damage-dealing items.

Single shot weapons

These weapons are traditional single-shot firearms, you fire one shot and then reload, some weapons have unique traits or are specifically designed for certain purposes

Musket

The basic Napoleonic firearm, a powerful and accurate shot, its bayonet becomes a free horde clearer if one masters it.

Carbine

An alternative weapon for the line infantry, trades the bayonet, accuracy, and power per shot for a quick reload, and zombie shoving. Recommended for players who want the reload of the pistol, but also wants the powerful sidearms of the line infantry.

Pistol

The default weapon for the officer, one of the quickest reloads, but low damage, inaccurate at a far range, good for hunting bombers from close to medium ranges.

Rifle

Derived from hunting rifles, high power per shot, but suffers from a slow reload time and walkspeed, allows its user to aim through its iron sights, allowing for easier long range sniping. No damage reduction against zappers, allowing for mostly one shot kills.
