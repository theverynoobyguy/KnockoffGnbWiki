Guts and Blackpowder
Guts and Blackpowder is a Roblox game developed by the Guts and Blackpowder group, the game was created on Janurary 31st, 2023 and is being actively developed. This wiki serves as a guide and information hub for anything present in or related to the game. This wiki is unofficial, and is created by GMAXnoob (me), and is being actively developed. If there are any suggestions you'd like to make, any bugs you'd like to report, or just basic questions or inquiries, please feel free to reach out to me via Discord, @gmaxnoob. This is my first website so some animations may look bad, the code/website unorganized, or there might be some glitches or issues (if so please contact me), but these things will be improved upon in the future, when my coding skills become better and more refined.
